Environment

Megh Foundations

To create environment awareness among the masses, schools, college students etc.by launching various environmental related surveys and data generation on continuous basis on pollutants for better planning. carrying out research and development into sustainable waste management practices and disseminating the useful results of that research and sustainable waste management practices include waste management, management of pollution and harm from waste, reduce of waste, recycling of waste, waste recovery activities and the clearing of pollutants from contaminated land and water body.

  • Date: May 2021
  • Category: Awareness

Education

Megh Foundation

To establish, run and manage schools, colleges, educational institutions, hostels (boys & girls), children care centres, rehabilitation centres and to organize computer courses and technical & vocational education with the help and aid from the Government agencies. Institutions, societies, trusts etc. And for the said purpose establish institutions, computer centres at villages, districts, state at all India level. To promote and encourage advancement of literary, cultural, political, religious & scientific education.

  • Date: May 2021
  • Category: Aid

Employment

Megh Foundation

To Provide employment opportunities by developing, promoting the arts, crafts, cottage and small-scale industries for poor and needy men & women. To impart instruction on and training in domestic hygiene, domestic economy, music, agriculture, carpentry, clay modelling, basket weaving, cane works spinning, bamboo work, tailoring and other cottage industries and to start Production-cum-Training Centre in different trade.

  • Date: May 2021
  • Category: Freshers

Skill development & Self improvement

Megh Foundation

To Conduct Seminars Regarding Anti-Social Events, Career Guidance, Talent Test, Competitive Exams Coaching, Self-Employment and all other Training Concerned to Youth. establish soft skill development programs and coaching classes also arrange various competition in school, colleges and training centres for develop inner skill of youth. To organize classes and competition of music, performing art, yoga & meditation, painting to give platforms of talented youth.

  • Date: May 2021
  • Category: Activities

Art & cultural progress

Megh Foundation

To organize Cultural, Folk Art, musical, Classical Training School and Colleges. To organize cultural programs, musical programs, performing arts programs, drama and other awareness programs and competition all over India. To promote and establish the traditional arts and crafts of India.

  • Date: May 2021
  • Category: Programs

Social Development & Human Rights

Megh Foundation

Arranging Seminars, workshops, training programs, symposia, lectures, debates, panel discussions, webinar, online conferences and courses on current research topics and other topics of international, national and local interest pertaining to research, training and applied aspects of social and development related issues. Protect human rights and raise voice against anti-social events and unfair to labours. And take such legal action for justice.

  • Date: May 2021
  • Category: Events
